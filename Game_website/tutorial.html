Tutorial

In a kingdom, the king had a princess who was old enough to wed. The king declared a competition to select his son-in-law. The contestants have to travel around the world to find and collect treasures. Along the way, there are not only treasures but also dangers. The person who has the most treasures will be allowed to marry the princess.

Goal

Open random boxes around the world to win the treasure! The player who gets the most treasures will have the chance to marry the princess.

Random Box

What would be inside the box? There could be huge amounts of treasure or huge bombs. Which box to open first is your choice. If you want to be a member of the royal family, you have to risk whatever is in the box and open it.

Control

You can adjust the movement of the airplane by pushing the arrow key buttons. If you want to open the box, move the airplane to the box and press Enter on the box.
